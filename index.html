Baby Food Selector

This selector will help you to create a menu for your baby that meets all of the requirments for nutrition in baby's first year.

  • Stage 1: This is the first baby food you're 4+ month year old will try. Always introduce one at a time for a few days before trying another. This makes monitoring for allergies easier.
  • Stage 2: These foods are still pureed but can be combined. Good for you 6-9 months old.
  • Stage 3: The last of the pureed food journey. These food introduce textures to your 9+ month old baby
